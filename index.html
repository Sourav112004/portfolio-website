About Me

I’m a BCA student at Bengaluru City University passionate about data analysis. My journey began when I worked on a small project during college that required understanding trends in student performance. I was fascinated by how much information we could uncover with just a few charts and tables. That moment sparked my curiosity and pushed me to explore tools like Power BI, Excel, and SQL.

Over time, I realized I love telling stories with data — turning raw numbers into insights that are easy to understand and meaningful to decision-makers. For me, data analysis is not just about numbers; it’s about uncovering the story behind them and using visuals to make that story impactful.
